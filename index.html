About the Page

Started in the year 2020, SL photography is an online photography page where the pictures taken by the photographer Shagun Lokre are being uploaded. All the pictures that are uploaded on the site are purely taken using mobile. This is a personal page where I showcase the pictures that I have taken throughout the year. This page consists of all kinds of photography work nature, sculptures, travel and etc. For me photography is not just a hobby, it's an emotion which I like to capture them and cherish the moment whenever I look at them. I started clicking pictures in the year 2017 and never stopped capturing the best moments. Till now I have captured almost 90 pictures and all of them were taken using mobile. Well, there's nothing much to say about me, I am an undergraduate student pursuing Bachelors of Technology in Computer Science Engineering. I hope you guys like my page.
